Learn More

We empower rescues and shelters to reach more potential adopters. Our platform provides powerful tools to manage applications, post adoptable pets, coordinate meet-and-greets, and track post-adoption progress. We handle the busywork so organizations can focus on the pets!

Happy Tails provides resources and a community to help pet owners properly care for their animals' health, nutrition, training, and overall wellbeing.

Happy Tails partners with local veterinarians to offer reduced rates and facilitate affordable access to essential medical care for adopted pets.

We are working for the community

At Happy Tails, we believe in the power of community to drive positive change. That's why engaging and collaborating with local animal welfare partners, volunteers, and pet lovers is core to our mission.

We also rely on passionate volunteers who generously donate their time and skills to support pets in need. From assisting at adoption events to providing photography services for adoptable pet profiles, our volunteers make lifesaving work possible.
